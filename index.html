This is the website of Rohan French, an Australian philosophical logician currently at large in Europe. I'm a Postdoctoral researcher on the Roots of Deduction project run by Catarina Dutilh Novaes in the Department of Theoretical Philosophy at the University of Groningen in the Netherlands. You can find out more about me here.

My CV→

My research focuses mostly on issues in modal logic and philosophical proof-theory. I have broad philosophical interests, and am particuarly interested in any issue in philosophy where formal methods can be fruitfully applied.

Previously I did my PhD in Australia under the supervision of the inimitable Lloyd Humberstone.
